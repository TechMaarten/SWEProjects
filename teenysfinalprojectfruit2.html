  • The combination of vitamin C and antioxidants in oranges promotes collagen production, contributing to healthy and vibrant skin. Oranges contain nutrients like vitamin C, potassium, and calcium, which contribute to bone health.

  • Pineapple is an excellent source of vitamin C. Bromelain in pineapple also has anti-inflammatory effects. Pineapple is a good source of dietary fiber.

  • Grapes are packed with powerful antioxidants. Grapes have a high water content, which helps keep the body hydrated. Resveratrol, found in grape skins, has been linked to heart health.
